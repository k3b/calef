Usually calendar entries are shared in the human-unreadable technical format "ICS" (or "VCS") that calendar
programs can read.

CalEF receives the technical format and re-sends/re-shares it-s content as human readable text.

Requirements:

<ul>
<li>Android-4.4 (api 19).</li>
<li>Permissions<<ul>
     <li>None</li>
     </ul>
/li>
</ul>
